About Mettu Infrastructures

With over 15 years of expertise in the construction industry, MettuInfra Constructions has established itself as a trusted name in delivering high-quality, innovative, and sustainable building solutions. Our portfolio spans across diverse sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects, each crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

We pride ourselves on our ability to turn complex challenges into successful outcomes, thanks to our team of skilled professionals and our adoption of the latest construction technologies. Whether it's a small renovation or a large-scale development, we bring the same level of dedication, precision, and passion to every project.

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead the construction industry towards a sustainable future by embracing cutting-edge technologies and environmentally responsible practices. We strive to set new benchmarks for quality and innovation in every project we undertake, with the goal of exceeding our clients' expectations and contributing to the betterment of the community.

We believe in creating structures that not only stand the test of time but also enhance the lives of those who use them, fostering a lasting positive impact on society and the environment.

Our Mission

At MettuInfra Constructions, our mission is to deliver top-tier construction services that prioritize customer satisfaction, innovation, and ethical standards. We are committed to building strong relationships with our clients by providing transparent communication, exceptional quality, and reliable performance.

Our focus on sustainability and community growth drives us to not only meet but exceed industry standards, ensuring that every project we complete contributes positively to the environment and the lives of our clients.

Our Construction Services & Specifications

At Mettu Infra Constructions, we offer a comprehensive range of construction services tailored to meet the highest industry standards. From structural integrity to finishing touches, our expertise spans all facets of building and renovation, ensuring exceptional quality and performance.

View All Services

Structural Engineering

Robust RCC framed structures designed to perfection. External walls are 9” thick and internal walls 4.5” thick, utilizing high-quality Kollur Bricks for durability.

Plastering

Expert plastering with a smooth sponge finish for both external and internal walls, ensuring a polished and refined appearance.

Flooring

High-quality vitrified tiles for indoor flooring and durable parking tiles for outdoor areas, providing both elegance and functionality.

Doors

Elegant teakwood main door frames and shutters with a fine polish, complemented by durable teakwood frames and flush doors for other areas.

Windows

Teakwood frames with premium pinhead glass shutters, providing both aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency.

Plumbing

Comprehensive plumbing solutions with options for Indian or European-style water closets. Tiles dadoing up to 7 feet and high-quality fittings for durability and convenience.

Electrical

Safe and efficient concealed copper wiring with multiple points. Includes telephone and TV points in the living and master bedroom for modern convenience.

Fixtures

Complete installation of ceiling fans, tube lights, air conditioners, and geysers in two bedrooms, ensuring a fully equipped and comfortable living space.

Ceiling

Modern POP false ceilings throughout the property, adding both style and functionality to every room.

Kitchen

Stylish granite countertops and sinks with ceramic tile dadoing up to 3 feet. Includes a chimney for efficient ventilation.

Painting

Smooth finishes with Birla Wall Care and emulsion paints. External elevations with emulsion paints and internal areas with high-quality cement-based paints.

Cupboards

High-quality plywood shutters with a sun-glass finish and teak wood frames with polish. Includes elegant paneling in the hall for added sophistication.

Security

Secure compound walls equipped with a sturdy iron gate, ensuring both privacy and safety for your property.

Owner's Cost

Transparent breakdown of additional costs including registration, VAT, labor, service charges, and any applicable government taxes.
